Malawax

Description

Malawax is a sweet, thin, and delicate Somali pancake that's similar to a crepe. It's often eaten for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingriedents

  • 2 cup plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and half cups milk
  • 1 and half cups water
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • honey for serving

Instructions

  1. In a blender combine flour, egg, milk, water, cardamom, salt and the butter, and blend until smooth
  2. Cover and set a side for 30 minutes
  3. Heat a non stick pan over medium heat and brush lightly with oil or butter
  4. Pour a little batter into the pan swirling to thinly cover the base
  5. Cook until crepes are golden underneath about one to two minutes
  6. Spread little oil or butter so that the crepes don't stick to the pan
  7. Then turnover and on the other side, repeat with the remaining batter
  8. Transfer into a plate and serve warm with butter and honey
