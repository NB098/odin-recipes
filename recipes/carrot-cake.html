Carrot cake

Description

Carrot cake is a sweet and moist spice cake, full of cut carrots and toasted nuts, and covered in cream cheese icing.

Ingredients:

Cake

  • 225ml sunflower oil
  • 5 medium eggs
  • 275g light brown sugar
  • 300g grated carrots
  • 100g raisins optional
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 275g self raising flour
  • 2 tsp mixed spice
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 100g walnuts/chopped pecans optional
    •

    Buttercream

  • 250g unsalted butter room temperature
  • 500g icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 - 3 tbsp boiling water
  • Chopped nuts
  • Carrot shapes/sprinkles
    •

    Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 180C/160C Fan
    2. Grease & line two 8" cake tins with parchment paper
    3. Pour the sunflower oil and eggs into a large bowl and add the light brown sugar
    4. Mix lightly and combine
    5. Add the grated carrots and raisins (if using) and orange zest and fold through to combine
    6. Add the self raising flour, mixed spice, ginger, and cinnamon and mix again - try not to over mix!
    7. Once combined, fold through the nuts (if using) and pour into the prepared tins
    8. Bake the cakes in the oven for 30-35 minutes until cooked through (test with a cake tester, should be clean!)
    9. Leave to cool in the tin

    Buttercream

    1. Beat the unsalted butter for a couple of minutes so its very smooth in a stand mixer with the paddle attachment
    2. Gradually add the icing sugar until all combined and beat on a high speed for a couple of minutes
    3. Once mixed, add the vanilla extract and mix again
    4. Whilst mixing, gradually add the boiling water until you reach your desired consistency
    5. Beat for another 5 minutes so its super light and fluffy
    6. Place one of your sponges onto the plate you're using, spread half of the buttercream on top, add the other sponge, and spread the rest of the buttercream on
    7. Decorate cake with carrot shapes
